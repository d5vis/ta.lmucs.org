## **Rules for Safety, Security, and Order**

### **Physical Safety**

- Follow posted safety guidelines and use protective equipment when required.
- Report unsafe conditions or hazards immediately to supervisors.

### **Protection of Equipment/Facilities**

- Use university equipment only for authorized purposes.
- Keep work areas clean and avoid actions that may damage facilities.
- **Failure to maintain clean, orderly, and sanitary conditions in any area, whether on-duty or off-duty, is grounds for immediate termination.**

### **Security of Customers and Staff**

- Display ID badges when on-duty and comply with security checks by LMU Public Safety when requested.
- Do not allow unauthorized individuals into restricted areas.

### **Prevention of Harassment or Violence**

- Refrain from abusive, threatening, or intimidating behavior.
- Maintain professional boundaries and treat everyone with respect.

These rules apply **any time** someone (TA or not) is in the lab.

## **Rules for Workplace Operations and Conduct**

### **Maintaining Smooth Operations**

- Avoid creating excessive noise or disturbances in work areas especially during business hours.
- Follow established procedures to prevent unnecessary interruptions.

### **Caring for Property**

- Handle all university property with care.
- Use tools and equipment according to instructions to prevent damage.
- **Failure to maintain clean, orderly, and sanitary conditions in any area, whether on-duty or off-duty, is grounds for immediate termination.**

### **Supporting TAs and Students**

- Do not obstruct others’ workspaces or impede their duties.
- Communicate and collaborate in a manner that supports others’ responsibilities.

### **Upholding Health and Safety Standards**

- Adhere to all hygiene, sanitation, and safety protocols.
- Comply with emergency procedures and evacuation rules.
- **Failure to maintain clean, orderly, and sanitary conditions in any area, whether on-duty or off-duty, is grounds for immediate termination.**
